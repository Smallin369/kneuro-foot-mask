  KNEURO — AI IMAGE GENERATION PROMPTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Use ChatGPT-4o image, Flux Kontext, Midjourney V7, or Imagen 3.
Always upload your real product photo as reference where possible.
Generate 6-8 candidates per prompt — pick the slightly imperfect
one (UGC look), discard the polished ones (look like ads).

NEVER generate faces if you'll caption them with names — deceptive
and Meta will reject the ads.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  HOW TO ATTACH A REFERENCE IMAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ChatGPT-4o image: drag the real product photo into the chat, then
  paste the prompt. Add: "Use the attached image as a reference for
  the exact product packaging, dimensions, and label design. Place
  it into the new scene below."

Flux Kontext (FAL.ai / Replicate): upload reference, prompt with
  "preserve the product packaging from the reference image, change
  only the background and hand placement to match this scene:"

Midjourney V7: upload reference, use --cref [URL] --cw 80
  Then prompt the scene. --cw 80 keeps strong character consistency.

Imagen 3 (Google AI Studio): upload reference image as input, use
  prompt below + add "match the product appearance from the input image"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  6. SIZE-ANCHORING TRICKS (when AI keeps making pouch too small)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Append any of these to a prompt to force larger packaging:

▪ "the pouch is approximately 15 centimeters wide and 20 centimeters
   tall, roughly the size of two iPhones placed side by side"

▪ "the pouch is large enough that holding it requires nearly an
   entire open palm, fingers visibly stretched around its edges"

▪ "the pouch is the same size as a typical face mask sheet packet
   from K-beauty brands like Mediheal or Innisfree, NOT a small tea
   sachet or sugar packet"

▪ "the pouch fills approximately 25 percent of the total square frame"

▪ "for scale reference, the pouch is taller than the hand holding it
   from base of palm to fingertip"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  WHAT NOT TO GENERATE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

❌ Faces with first names captioned ("Sarah, 32") — deceptive
❌ "Before" feet with cracked or severe skin — Meta auto-flag
❌ Feet showing dramatic peeling — confuses your "no peel" position
❌ Studio-lit perfectly composed product shots
❌ Multiple people in scene with faces — implied testimonials
❌ Tea-sachet-sized pouch — your real product is envelope-sized
