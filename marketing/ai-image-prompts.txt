USE THESE PROMPTS TO REPLACE THE PRODUCT-MOCKUP HERO IMAGES
WITH UGC-STYLE LIFESTYLE PHOTOGRAPHY. Generates 4-8 candidates
per prompt. Pick the most "imperfect" looking one — the slightly-
off-balance ones read as authentic; the pristine ones read as ads.

NEVER GENERATE IMAGES WITH FACES if you intend to caption them
with a fake name or location. That's deceptive and Meta will
reject the ads.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  5. WHAT NOT TO GENERATE (do not use these)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

❌ Faces with first names captioned ("Sarah, 32") — deceptive
❌ "Before" feet with cracked/severe skin — Meta will auto-flag as
   medical/before-after content and reject ads
❌ Feet showing dramatic peeling — confuses your "no peel" positioning
❌ Studio-lit perfectly composed product shots — already what you have
❌ Multiple people in scene with faces — implied testimonials

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  GENERATOR-SPECIFIC TIPS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

▪ ChatGPT-4o (GPT Plus, $20/mo)
  Best for lifestyle "iPhone-style" outputs. Append:
  "shot on iPhone, no professional retouching, slight motion blur,
   imperfect framing, candid"
  Run 4-6 generations, throw out the "studio" ones, keep the messy.

▪ Flux Pro (FAL.ai, ~$0.05/image)
  Strongest realistic skin/hand rendering as of 2026. Use Flux 1.1
  Pro Ultra. Set steps=30, guidance=3.5, aspect_ratio=1:1.

▪ Midjourney V7 (~$10/mo)
  Beautiful but over-stylized. ALWAYS append:
  --style raw --stylize 50 --ar 1:1 --v 7
  Without --style raw it'll look like a magazine ad.

▪ Imagen 3 (free, Google AI Studio at ai.google.dev)
  Surprisingly good for flat-lay product shots. Free tier is generous.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  SELECTION CHECKLIST (pick the right gen)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

For each generation, score against these criteria:

☐ Looks like a real phone photo, not a studio ad?
☐ Lighting is natural and slightly imperfect?
☐ Composition is slightly off-center, not perfectly framed?
☐ No visible AI artifacts on the pouch text or hand fingers?
   (Hands and faces are where AI fails most — count fingers!)
☐ Color palette matches Kneuro brand (cream, beige, warm-white)?
☐ Aspect ratio is 1:1 (square)?
☐ Resolution at least 1200x1200 for sharp display on retina?

If 5 or more boxes are checked, ship it.
If fewer, regenerate.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  AFTER YOU GENERATE — UPLOAD WORKFLOW
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Pick the winning image at 1024x1024 or higher.
2. Open it in Preview/Photoshop and lightly downscale to 1200x1200
   if needed (sharper than 4K for web — server compression handles
   the rest).
3. Save as JPEG quality 85 or PNG (Shopify CDN converts to WebP
   automatically since we set format: 'webp' in the theme).
4. Shopify Admin → Settings → Files → Upload (drag-drop).
5. Right-click the uploaded file → Copy URL.
6. Online Store → Customize → Home page → Advertorial Hero section
   → Lead image → "Replace" → paste URL or browse to the file.
7. Save. Hard-refresh (Cmd+Shift+R) the live page to confirm.
8. Repeat for /pages/about, /pages/ingredients, /pages/kneuro-vs-baby-foot
   if you want them all using new imagery.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ETHICAL DISCLOSURE NOTE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Some jurisdictions are tightening rules on AI-generated content in
advertising. Best practice as of 2026:

▪ Lifestyle / product-in-scene imagery does NOT require disclosure.
▪ Anything implying a real customer story DOES — or you risk FTC
  attention in the US, ASA in the UK, etc.
▪ Always source TESTIMONIAL photos from real customers. Pay them
  $10-25 store credit + a thank-you. Don't fake it.
