VOICEOVER — KNEURO FOOT MASK AD V1
Funny / intrigue hook framework. Time-locked to your 19s a-roll v2.

——————————————————————————

CLEAN SCRIPT (read straight through)

My pedicurist gave up on me. Look why. Same feet, different week. Foot mask, thirty minutes, both feet. Soft, smooth, no more cracks. My pedicurist booked me back in.

——————————————————————————

WHY THIS HOOK WORKS

"My pedicurist gave up on me" stops scrolls because:

1. It's a CONFESSION with a STORY built in (5 words, full character + stakes)
2. Universal pain (every woman in your audience has been to a pedicure)
3. Implies real consequence (a professional refused you)
4. Funny but believable
5. Sets up a curiosity gap: "How bad were they that the pro quit?"

The closing line "My pedicurist booked me back in" BOOKENDS the joke — pedicurist quit at the start, took her back at the end. That's a full narrative arc in 14 seconds AND it doubles as social proof.
