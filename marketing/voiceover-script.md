# Voiceover Script — Foot Mask Ad v1

**Style:** Conversational. Like you're texting your best friend about something gross-but-amazing. NOT advertising voice.

---

## THE 7 LINES

1. **"Okay, if your heels look like this... you HAVE to try this."**
2. **"It's a goat milk foot mask. One hour."**
3. **"Step one — slip 'em on."**
4. **"Both feet. Sit back, watch a show, whatever."**
5. **"Take the sticker off, peel them off — dead skin everywhere."**
6. **"Rinse it. And look at this."**
7. **"Soft baby feet. Link in bio."**

---

## Mood per line

| Line | Feel |
|---|---|
| 1 | Showing a friend something gross and exciting |
| 2 | Flat, factual — explaining the basics |
| 3 | Casual, no enthusiasm yet |
| 4 | Slight smile on "whatever" — relaxed |
| 5 | Mild disgust + delight on "everywhere" |
| 6 | Quiet, almost whispered |
| 7 | Smug, intimate — you fixed it |

---

## How to record

1. Bathroom with a towel on the floor (kills echo)
2. Phone Voice Memos, 6 inches from your mouth
3. **Each line 3 times separately** — pick best in editing
4. Re-record anything that sounds rehearsed

---

## Banned

- "Amazing", "incredible", "obsessed"
- Influencer upspeak at line endings
- "Hey guys" / "Hey girls"
- Forced enthusiasm

If a line sounds like an ad — redo it.
