# Kneuro Foot Mask — Ad Creative v1

**Platform:** Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
**Format:** 9:16 vertical UGC-style video, 13–15s
**Product:** Hydrating goat milk foot mask. Clear sock with serum inside. 30 min wear. Instant soft, hydrated, crack-free feet.
**Audience:** Women 25–45 with cracked heels, hide their feet, can't wear sandals confidently.
**Strict rule:** Zero peel/exfoliation language. This is a HYDRATION product.

---

## ANGLES (4 to test)

| # | Angle | Why it works |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Pain-Bridge** — "If your heels look like mine used to" | Viewer identifies themselves first, then sees you on the other side |
| 2 | **Failed-Solutions** — "Pedicures and urea cream barely worked" | Listing what they've already tried earns credibility |
| 3 | **Speed Outcome** — "Soft hydrated feet in 30 minutes" | Specific timeline + instant gratification |
| 4 | **Confession** — "I haven't worn sandals in years" | Vulnerability hook, real consequence |

already in the sock. Thirty minutes, take it off — soft, hydrated, no
cracks. I'm on my third box.

## TEST PLAN

Run **3 ads in parallel** (one per hook variant) for 72 hours at $20/day each. Total spend: $180.

After 72hrs, evaluate:
- Hook rate (3-second view rate) — which hook is stopping scrolls?
- CTR — which is driving the click?
- LP→ATC % — which audience is converting on the LP?

Kill the bottom 1, scale the top 1, test new variations of the winning angle.
