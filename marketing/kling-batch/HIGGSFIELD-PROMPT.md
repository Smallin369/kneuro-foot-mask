## WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE

When the clip plays:
- Camera stays locked at the same downward angle the whole time, no shake
- Both feet visible the entire 5 seconds
- Feet barely move — slight weight shift, micro toe wiggle
- Sleeves stay perfectly fitted on the feet, no sliding or morphing
- Floor stays consistent — no surface changes
- No hands, no body parts other than the feet/calves, no face
- Lighting stays consistent throughout

If those 7 things are true, this is a hit. Use it.
